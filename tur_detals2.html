“Convenient if you frequently use the metro but bring ear plugs”

This is a modest hotel whose main attraction is the 4 to 5 minute walking distance to the Corentin Celton station on the no. 12 metro line. It is also quite close to the town of Issy with some nice restaurants (such as La Scala), but for the rest it has little to offer.
