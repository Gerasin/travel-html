“Convenient if you frequently use the metro but bring ear plugs”

This is a modest hotel whose main attraction is the 4 to 5 minute walking distance to the Corentin Celton station on the no. 12 metro line. It is also quite close to the town of Issy with some nice restaurants (such as La Scala), but for the rest it has little to offer.

Even though the building seems to be originally constructed as a hotel, the inside walls seem to be made of paperboard: the rooms are extremely noisy. And very very small. The good news is that the traffic noise from the busy roads on either side of the hotel are less disturbing. Also breakfast is quite decent (the usual croissants and coffee, but also yoghurt and decent orange juice)
